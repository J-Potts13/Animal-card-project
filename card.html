African Bush Elephant

African bush elephant is the largest land mammal in the world and the largest of the three elephant species.

  • Scientific Name: Loxodonta Africana
  • Average Length: 24 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 60-70 years
  • Habitat: Open Savanna to the desert

Both male and female have visiable tusks. Their large ears are shaped like the continent of Africa. An elephants trunk has over 40,000 muscles and tentden that can liflift more then 400 pounds.
